## 🔹 5) MAIN CONTENT STRUCTURE

> ⚠️ Use ONLY H2 & H3
> ⚠️ Headings: **3–6 words max** — short, noun-phrase style, NO filler words
> ⚠️ NO question-style H2 headings (e.g., "How to...", "Why You Should...")
> ⚠️ NO padded headings (e.g., "A Complete Guide to...", "Everything About...")
> ⚠️ Pattern: `[Topic] + [Game/App Name] + [Modifier]`
> ⚠️ Native English — clear, direct, conversion-focused

**✅ Good (follow sample article style):**
- `Key Features of [Game] Mod APK`
- `MOD Features Advantage`
- `Installation Guide [Game] Mod APK`
- `Tips for Playing [Game]`

**❌ Bad:**
- `A Comprehensive Overview of All the Key Features`
- `Why You Should Download This MOD Version Today`
- `How to Install the APK on Your Android Device`
